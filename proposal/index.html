Outline

Graphics layer

The most interesting and most work intensive part of the project. The graphics layer will be built in three.js. It will boast several features:

  • Square room model with open ceiling
  • Diffuse, ambient, and specular lighting (marble floors and walls)
  • Texture mapping of artist's images on 3d frames
  • Texture mapping of a cloudy sky, as a source of diffuse lighting
  • Texture mapping of a constant statue in the middle of the room
  • At least 4 camera angles - one facing each image, and one perspective of the whole room
  • TBD, constrained camera movement animation

Application, data, and storage

The part that makes the project useful and fun - anyone can use it for free, and share their galleries with the world. The following technology stack is used

  • Express.js webserver hosted on Amazon EC2 cloud compute instance
  • Angular.js client-side application
  • Firebase document datastore, connected directly to the client
  • Amazon S3 for image storage, connected directly to the client
